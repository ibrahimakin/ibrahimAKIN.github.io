Overview

Gift Balance (the “App”) is provided by ibrahim AKIN at no cost and is intended to be used as is. The app uses the selected supermarket's API to retrieve balance inquiry results.

We respect your privacy. This App does not collect, store, or share any personal or device data. There are no accounts, analytics, advertising SDKs, trackers, or cookies.

Information Collection and Use

The App only uses the selected supermarket's API to obtain balance inquiry results. No data from your device or browsing activity is transmitted to the developer or stored by the developer. Any requests to the selected supermarket's API are for the sole purpose of providing the App’s core functionality.

Data Sharing

Because we do not collect data, we do not share any data with third parties, service providers, advertisers, affiliates, or analytics platforms.

Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, or from any user.

Security

Since the App does not handle user data, there is no personal information stored on our servers that requires protection. To ensure authenticity and integrity, download the App only from official app stores where we publish it.
